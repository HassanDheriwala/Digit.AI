— PROCESS —

How It Works

Three elegant steps from canvas to prediction.

01

Draw Your Digit

Use the interactive canvas to draw any digit from 0 to 9. The canvas captures your stroke in real time at 280×280 pixels.

02

CNN Processes Image

The image is resized to 28×28 pixels, normalized, and fed through 2 convolutional layers with MaxPooling and a Dense output layer.

03

AI Predicts Result

Softmax activation outputs probabilities for all 10 digits. The highest confidence prediction is returned instantly with a full probability breakdown.
